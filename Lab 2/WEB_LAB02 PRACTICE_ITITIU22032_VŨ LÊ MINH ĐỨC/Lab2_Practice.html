Our Mission

San Joaquin Valley Town Hall is a non-profit organization that is run by an all-voluntary board of direction. Our mission is to bring nationally and internationally renowned, thought-provoking speakers who inform, educate and entertain our audience! As one of our members once told us:

"Each year I give a ticket package to each of our family members. I think of it as the gift of knowledge... and that is priceless"
